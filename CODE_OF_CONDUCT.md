# 贡献者行为准则 / Contributor Code of Conduct

## 我们的承诺  
为营造一个开放、包容、友好且健康的社区环境，作为贡献者和维护者，我们承诺尊重每一位参与者，无论其学历、年龄、性别、性取向、残障状况、体型、民族、国籍、种族、宗教、政治立场、地域、社会经济地位、文化背景等身份属性。  
我们反对任何形式的歧视，包括但不限于学历歧视、性别歧视、年龄歧视、种族歧视、性取向歧视、宗教歧视等。

## Our Pledge
In order to build an open, inclusive, friendly, and healthy community, as contributors and maintainers we pledge to respect every participant, regardless of their educational background, age, gender, sexual orientation, disability, body type, ethnicity, nationality, race, religion, political views, location, socioeconomic status, or cultural background.  
We are opposed to all forms of discrimination, including but not limited to educational, gender, age, racial, sexual orientation, and religious discrimination.

---

## 我们的准则  
我们鼓励并倡导如下行为：  
- 尊重并善待每一位参与者  
- 用包容、正面的语言沟通交流  
- 理解与接纳不同观点和经验  
- 乐于接受正向、建设性的建议或批评  
- 主动为社区其他成员提供帮助  
- 推动和维护社区的开放与自由

同时，社区内禁止以下行为：  
- 任何形式的歧视、骚扰和攻击，包括针对学历、性别、年龄、种族、国籍、残障、性取向、信仰等的贬低、侮辱、威胁、孤立等  
- 故意制造冒犯、恐吓或骚扰  
- 公布他人个人信息、隐私  
- 骚扰、跟踪或持续制造不适者  
- 不尊重不同的声音及自由表达权  
- 破坏社区自由、平等、公正氛围的任何行为

## Our Standards  
Examples of encouraged behavior include:  
- Respecting and treating every participant kindly  
- Using inclusive and positive language in communication  
- Understanding and accepting different views and experiences  
- Being receptive to constructive suggestions or criticism  
- Proactively helping other community members  
- Promoting and defending openness and freedom in the community

The following behaviors are prohibited in our community:  
- Any form of discrimination, harassment, or attack, including denigration, insult, threat, or isolation based on education, gender, age, race, nationality, disability, sexual orientation, or faith  
- Intentional offense, intimidation, or harassment  
- Disclosing others' personal information or privacy  
- Harassment, stalking, or repeated discomfort towards others  
- Disrespecting different voices and freedom of expression  
- Any behavior that undermines the community's free, equal, and just atmosphere

---

## 维护者的责任  
维护者有责任和权利监督社区氛围，及时制止、处理违反准则的行为。社区维护者有权对无视、违反本行为准则的参与者进行警告、移除贡献、甚至临时或永久封禁其账号等必要处理措施。

## Maintainers’ Responsibilities  
Maintainers are responsible for overseeing the community atmosphere and taking timely action against behavior that violates the code of conduct. Community maintainers have the right to warn, remove contributions, and even temporarily or permanently ban contributors who ignore or violate this code of conduct.

---

## 报告与申诉  
如您在社区内遇到违反本准则的行为或其他不适事件，请及时通过以下方式反馈：  
- 邮箱联系：arab@methodbox.top  
- 或在 [GitHub Issues](https://github.com/ArabidopsisDev/ReciteHelper/issues) 区留言  
我们承诺对所有举报信息严格保密，并公正高效地处理您的诉求。

## Reporting and Appeals  
If you encounter violations of this code or other inappropriate incidents in the community, please report them through:  
- Email: arab@methodbox.top  
- Or submit an issue in the [GitHub Issues](https://github.com/ArabidopsisDev/ReciteHelper/issues) area  
We promise strict confidentiality and fair, prompt handling of all reports.

---

## 自由与开放的承诺  
我们的社区致力于保障成员的基本自由，包含思想、言论、表达、技术创新等自由，任何人不得以任何理由剥夺他人基本权利。我们鼓励包容和多元，反对封闭和压制。

## Commitment to Freedom and Openness  
Our community is committed to protecting the fundamental freedoms of its members, including freedom of thought, speech, expression, and technical innovation. No one shall deprive others of these basic rights for any reason. We encourage inclusiveness and diversity, and oppose closure and suppression.
